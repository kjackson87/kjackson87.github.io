Hey there! I'm Kyle, a Principal Applied Science Lead focused on AI safety and LLM evaluation systems. I'm currently leading efforts at Microsoft to develop sophisticated LLM-based safety evaluations and automated red-teaming solutions.

I've spent the last few years deep in the world of responsible AI, building systems that help detect and prevent harmful content generation while pushing the boundaries of what's possible with language models.

What I'm Working On

At Microsoft, I lead a team of brilliant research scientists where we're tackling some fascinating challenges:

I've been heavily involved in developing "LLM-Judges" that can automatically evaluate potentially harmful content across Microsoft's AI products. Our work on this was published in A Framework for Automated Measurement of Responsible AI Harms in Generative AI Applications.

Recently, I supervised research on prompt injection mitigations, which will be appearing in NAACL 2025 as Defense against Prompt Injection Attacks via Mixture of Encodings.

I'm particularly proud of our work on automated red-teaming and synthetic data generation platforms that help us proactively identify risks in generative models before they become problems.
